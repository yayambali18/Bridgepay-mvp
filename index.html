How It Works

🧑‍💻

Buyer

Buyer makes purchase and payment is held securely by BridgePay in escrow. No funds go directly to the seller until verification is complete.

🏪

Seller

Seller uploads timestamped proof (photos/video) with product details and ships the item. BridgePay verifies authenticity before payment release.

🤖

BridgePay AI

AI fingerprint detection compares seller proof with buyer's received product. If verified, payment releases. If disputed, investigation triggers and trust scores update.
